About Us

Value is our number one priority, Period.

Our history

Taylor and Oak is a company based in the heart of Norway not far from its capital, Oslo. We combine Scandinavian design with English timelessness. The result is beautiful products with a modern and warm design. Since beginning our journey back in 2019 our vision has been to provide customers with top quality products at a great price. We understand what it's like being a customer. This is why we feel so strongly about giving our supporters the value and support that they rightly deserve, so that life can be that much more enjoyable and easy.

We believe in making life easier,
so you have more time to enjoy yours.

Our Values

At Taylor & Oak we like to go by our 4 values that stand strong in the work that we do.

  • One collective, one adventure

    We believe that we are on the face of the Earth to create great products for people all over the world. We are all walking on this Earth together and we all benefit from helping eachother by sharing our gifts and talents. What we at Taylor & Oak do best is to create great products.

  • Making a difference

    Together we can make a positive difference in the world. Every single person and company has the power to create a positive difference in the world. This is a responsibility we take on fully. How we create a positive difference is by supplying great products at great prices, by fully supporting our customers. We ourselves have experienced getting both amazing customer service and vice versa. We know how much of a difference feeling supported as a customer means. We honour that customers are real people with real and busy lives, and pride ourselves with getting back to customers swiftly ensuring first class customer service.

  • Products we believe in

    We are a company with a Norwegian and English background. We take inspiration from this when we decide on our products, as we always choose products we ourselves as customers would love. Something we have learned from Scandinavian design is the power of simplicity, while we also draw inspiration from the classic yet cozy English design. This results in us creating products that can be enjoyed by anyone who likes simplicity with a homely twist.

  • Focus on what matters

    What matters the most to us is providing for our customers. We love and respect every single customer and show this by creating excellent products and suppoting you as well as we can. We see ourselves and our customers as a community, and look forward to growing together and really getting to know eachother.
